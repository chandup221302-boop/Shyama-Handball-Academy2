ADMISSION PROCESS

Follow these simple steps to become a proud member of the Shyama Handball Academy.

1

Apply Online

Fill out the online registration form below with your accurate details.

2

Trial & Evaluation

Attend a 2-day trial session for physical and tactical assessment by coaches.

3

Document Review

Submit required documents and medical clearance at the academy office.

4

Fee Payment

Complete the fee payment to officially secure your spot in the squad.

Basic Eligibility

  • Age Limit: Must be between 10 and 22 years of age at the time of admission.
  • Fitness: Must pass the academy's basic physical endurance test.
  • Medical: Must provide a certified medical fitness certificate.
  • Experience: Beginners to advanced players are welcome (Batches assigned accordingly).
