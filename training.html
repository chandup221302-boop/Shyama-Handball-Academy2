Our Training Programs

Beginner Training

Focuses on fundamental skills, rules of the game, and basic physical conditioning for newcomers.

Advanced Training

Intensive sessions covering advanced tactics, strategic gameplay, and high-performance drills.

Boys & Girls Teams

Dedicated team training for both boys and girls, focusing on team chemistry and match play.

Summer Camp

Special intensive training camps during summer vacations to boost skills rapidly.

Fitness & Conditioning

Specialized strength, agility, and endurance training tailored specifically for handball athletes.

School Programs

Partnership programs with local schools to introduce and develop handball at the grassroots level.
