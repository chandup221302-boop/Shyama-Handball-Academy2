Elite & Competitive

Fitness & Conditioning Program

Science-backed athletic conditioning designed specifically for handball players — building explosive power, endurance, agility, and injury resilience across three core modules.

Strength & Power

Periodized resistance training targeting explosive leg drive, throwing velocity, and core stability — the physical foundations of elite handball performance.

Speed & Agility

Ladder drills, reactive agility cones, and plyometrics designed to improve first-step quickness, directional change, and court-specific movement efficiency.

Endurance & Recovery

Aerobic base building combined with active recovery protocols, flexibility work, and nutrition guidance to keep athletes performing at peak across full seasons.
