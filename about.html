OUR STORY & IDENTITY

ABOUT SHYAMA HANDBALL ACADEMY

Shyama Handball Academy stands as one of Uttar Pradesh's most dedicated institutions for promoting handball at the grassroots level. Founded with the belief that every child deserves access to world-class sports training, we have been shaping young athletes into disciplined, confident, and technically proficient players for over a decade.

Rooted in the cultural heartland of Varanasi, our academy blends traditional values of hard work and integrity with modern coaching methodology. We operate under the trusted umbrella of S.N. Pandey Khel Sansthan Trust, ensuring governance, transparency, and a long-term commitment to the development of sports in the region.

From beginners discovering the sport for the first time to seasoned players competing at state and national levels, Shyama Handball Academy is the home for every handball dream.

WHERE WE ARE HEADED

VISION & MISSION

Our Vision

To become the premier handball development hub of North India — producing athletes of national stature while fostering a culture of sportsmanship, discipline, and community pride from the very foundations of grassroots sport.

Our Mission

To provide accessible, expert-led handball training to youth of all backgrounds; to instill values of teamwork, perseverance, and respect; and to build a sustainable sports ecosystem that opens doors to careers in athletics and beyond.
