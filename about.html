About Us

Our Vision & Mission

To cultivate a culture of excellence in handball by providing world-class training, fostering sportsmanship, and empowering athletes to compete at national and international levels. We aim to make Varanasi a hub for handball talent.

Founder's Message

"Sport is not just about physical strength; it's about discipline, teamwork, and character. We established this academy to give the youth of Varanasi the platform they deserve. Our commitment is to nurture talent and create champions of tomorrow."

- Founder, Shyama Handball Academy

Under the Aegis of S.N. Pandey Khel Sansthan Trust

The Shyama Handball Academy is proudly supported and operated under the aegis of the S.N. Pandey Khel Sansthan Trust. The trust is dedicated to the promotion of sports and youth development in the region, providing the necessary infrastructure and backing to ensure our athletes have everything they need to succeed.

Infrastructure & Facilities

  • Professional Grade Handball Courts
  • Advanced Fitness & Weight Training Center
  • Changing Rooms & Lockers
  • Medical & Physiotherapy Support

Why Choose Us?

  • Experienced and Certified Coaches
  • Regular Tournament Participation
  • Focus on Holistic Athlete Development
