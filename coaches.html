Shri Rajesh Kumar Pandey

Varanasi, U.P. 18+ Years Experience SAI Certified Level-3 500+ Athletes Trained

Shri Rajesh Kumar Pandey is the founding head coach and driving force behind Shyama Handball Academy. With over 18 years of dedicated service to handball development in Uttar Pradesh, Coach Pandey has built a training methodology that seamlessly blends classical Indian coaching values with modern sport science principles. A former national-level player himself, he brings first-hand competitive experience to every session, understanding precisely what it takes to perform under pressure. His vision of accessible, world-class handball training for every child — regardless of background — is the very soul of this academy.
